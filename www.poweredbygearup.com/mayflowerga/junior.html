Junior Member Application

A child between the ages of birth through age eighteen may become a Junior Member by being sponsored by a GSMD relative in the direct line. Lineage does not have to be proven for the Junior; however, the sponsor must have a proven lineage and fill out the application as the sponsor. The sponsor must include their GSMD and state number along with the sponsors ancestor.

Make checks payable to: The Georgia Mayflower Society for the amount of $40.00. Please write the name of the Junior Member in the memo line with the term “Junior Member”.

Send check and application to: Peggy McCall, Georgia Mayflower Education Chair
