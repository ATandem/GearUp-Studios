The Georgia Society of Mayflower Descendants is a state society chartered under the General Society of Mayflower Descendants. It is located in Atlanta Georgia and has 363 members. The chapter holds luncheon meetings twice a year in November and March in Atlanta at the Dunwoody Country Club.
