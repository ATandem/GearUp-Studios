CATALINA

This cruising boat is an all around day sailing boat. It is designed for a family of four to go cruising for a day. It is not fast but, extremely reliable and safe. It can be sailed by one person. It may be powered by an outboard motor. It can be raced but is not designed for it, it is designed more for comfort and enjoyment. It has a small deck cabin.
